About Me


I was born in Santa Cruz, Bolivia with aspirations to become an engineer. At a young age I
enjoyed tinkering and creating things to appease my curiosity. It wasn’t until high school
that I was first introduced to programming. My Track and Field coach persuaded me to take
my very first Computer Science class. Like anything in life, when I first started, I wasn’t
very good, but I decided to stick with Computer Science. Overtime, I grew to understand the
importance and uniqueness of programming. Everything anyone could possibly imagine could be
done with just the right idea, attitude and patience. I decided to pursue my bachelor’s in
Computer Science at Virginia Tech.Now a new chapter in my life has started and I’m looking
forward to seeing what the future holds.
